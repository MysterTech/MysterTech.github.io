WORK.
As the __principle lead/software consultant__ I established POC and re-designed implementation architecture for a data driven product. Result was a simple and responsive UI with special focus on data relevance and scope.
While the organisation was creating market space for virtual infrastructure management products, as a __Lead Engineer__, I was building Webservice APIs part of key SDKs including VSphere Management SDK and VMware Cloud Director. Also part of the role was documentation and consulting as __API expert__ for frontend developers within the organization.
Joined the team as an entry level engineer worked on migrating legacy healthcare data & management systems to web based interfaces. __Recognized__ and __awarded__ by clients for __improved appeal and usability__ of __SOARIAN Clinicals__ which established monopoly in the heathcare space later was __acquired by CERNER__.
At Axis Bank I worked on 3rd party integration APIs for the bank's Loyalty Management System(LMS). I joined as senior software engineer but was __promoted__ to leading the team and single POC for clients __within a couple of months__ owing to single handed effort in __design and implementation of REST based interfaces__. Other notable contributions were implementation of __industry standard documentation and coding practices__.
Armed with formidable years of experience as a java developer joined the team as __Technology Expert/Quantitative Analyst__. Led a team of creatively motivated engineers to create __performant, high frequency algorithmic trading bots__. While initial workflows were manually designed by experienced prop traders, later branched into __AI/ML based models__.
